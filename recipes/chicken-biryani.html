Chicken Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the royal khansamas of the durbar of Old Delhi, under the Mughal Empire, during the late 16th century of the erstwhile Mughal Court.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Aromatics - Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Herbs - Cilantro and Mint
  • Spices - Garam Masala and Ground Cinnamon
  • Red Onion
  • Ghee
  • Chilli Peppers
  • Basmati Rice

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken by mixing it with aromatics and herbs and let it rest for 1 hour.
  2. In a pot, saute the onions in ghee and set it aside
  3. Clean the rice using cold running water
  4. Par boil the rice for 7 minutes
